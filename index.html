The Charminar,Hyderabad

The Charminar is a historic monument located in Hyderabad, India, built in 1591 by Sultan Muhammad Quli Qutb Shah. It has four grand arches and is a symbol of the city's rich history and culture.

The Birla Mandir , Hydrabad

Birla Mandir is a famous Hindu temple located in Hyderabad, India, dedicated to Lord Venkateshwara. It is built with white marble and sits on a hill, offering panoramic views of the city.

The Tank Bund, Hydrabad

The Tank Bund is a scenic road in Hyderabad, built across Hussain Sagar Lake. It connects the twin cities of Hyderabad and Secunderabad and is lined with 33 statues of notable figures. The area is a popular spot for evening walks and boating activities.

Salar Jung Museum, Hydrabad

The Salar Jung Museum in Hyderabad is one of the largest art museums in India. It houses an extensive collection of art, antiques, and rare artifacts from around the world. The museum is renowned for its unique exhibits, including sculptures, manuscripts, and textiles.

The Ramoji Film City, Hydrabad
